# Code of Conduct

This project is committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for everyone.

## Expected behaviour

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and ask for clarification when needed.
- Focus on the work and the people who maintain it.

## Unacceptable behaviour

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, spamming, or sustained disruption.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness a problem, please contact the maintainer at: satsuma.fizzle_0u@icloud.com.
